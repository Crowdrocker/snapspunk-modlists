## Introduction

SNAPSPUNK is a pair of hand-curated, stable modlists focused on **story-driven gameplay** with **tactical combat**.  
- **Cyberpunk 2077** → Chrome & Blood inspired but with your personal cosmetics, Atelier shops, and body-type support.  
- **Fallout 4** → Magnum Opus inspired + optional Sim Settlements 2.

Both lists are built for **very hard / tactical** play. Builds matter. Nothing is handed to you.

**Current versions:**  
- SNAPSPUNK-2077 v1.0 (EBB & Solo Body)  
- SNAPSPUNK-FO4 v1.0 (Base & SS)

## Profiles

### Cyberpunk 2077
- **SNAPSPUNK-2077 EBB** – Enhanced Big Breasts body  
- **SNAPSPUNK-2077 Solo** – -KS- The Solo Body  

**Same core gameplay** — only body replacers, presets, clothing, and Atelier shops differ.

### Fallout 4
- **SNAPSPUNK-FO4 Base** – Pure Magnum Opus-style story & tactical experience (no settlements)  
- **SNAPSPUNK-FO4 SS** – Base + full Sim Settlements 2 integration
